# Product Vision: Votica Local SEO & Review Router

## The Core Mission
To provide local businesses in Tier 1 cities (starting in Hyderabad) with a frictionless, automated system to capture authentic, keyword-rich 5-star Google Reviews, while privately intercepting negative feedback before it hits the public web.

## The Strategy: The "Trojan Horse"
This product acts as a high-value, low-friction entry point for Votica Labs. By selling a tangible hardware/software solution at an impulse-buy price point (₹1,999), we acquire local B2B clients instantly. The recurring "review quota top-up" builds long-term MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) and establishes a warm channel to upsell custom app development, web design, and digital agency services.

## Operational Philosophy: The One-Call Close
Eliminate deployment delays. The entire system is built on a multi-tenant, zero-deploy architecture. A field agent must be able to pitch the product, input tenant details into the Admin Portal, instantly generate the subdomain, print the QR sticker on the spot, and hand over the live standee in under 15 minutes.
